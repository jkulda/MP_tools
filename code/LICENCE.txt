
												MP_TOOLS v.1.56 (November_2023)
												 
Computing tools for visualisation and analysis of atomic scale models of crystalline solids.
Ultra-fast generation of elastic and inelastic neutron and Xray diffuse scattering intensity  
maps in the momentum-energy space (Q,w) employing the non-uniform fast Fourier transform.
